Biography

Dr. Amita Arora is presently working as an Assistant Professor at Department of Computer Engineering in J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA Faridabad. She has fifteen years of experience in teaching. She has supervised ten M, Tech Thesis. Her current research interests are Machine Learning, Semantic Web,Information Retrieval, Natural language Processing. She has been teaching subjects like Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Analysis and Design of Algorithm, Computer Graphics.She has published Fifteen articles in International/National Journals and Conferences.
